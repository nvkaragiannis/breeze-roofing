### `/areas/[city]-nc/` — Service Area Pages

**Each page must have unique content. Template structure:**

**H1:** "Roofing Contractor [City], NC | Breeze Roofing"
**Meta Title:** "Roofing Contractor [City] NC | Roof Replacement & Repair | Breeze Roofing"

**Sections:**
1. Intro: We serve [City] homeowners... (mention specific neighborhoods/landmarks)
2. Common roofing challenges in [City] (coastal, inland — make it specific)
3. Our services in [City]
4. Local reviews from [City] customers (filter by location if possible)
5. [City]-specific zip codes served
6. CTA + estimate embed

**City-specific notes:**
- **Wilmington:** Downtown, Midtown, neighborhoods (Ogden, Porters Neck, Landfall, Mayfair). Largest page, most content.
- **Hampstead:** Growing community, lots of new construction, Pender County. Mention Topsail HS area.
- **Leland:** Fast-growing suburb, Brunswick County, new developments.
- **Carolina Beach:** Beachfront properties, vacation homes, salt air emphasis.
- **Wrightsville Beach:** Luxury oceanfront, premium materials, strict HOA compliance.
- **Southport:** Historic waterfront, older homes + restoration, Oak Island proximity.
- **Topsail Island:** Island roofing, wind exposure, coastal barrier requirements.
- **Surf City:** Similar to Topsail, Pender/Onslow county line.
