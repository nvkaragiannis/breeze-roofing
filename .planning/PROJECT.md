# Breeze Roofing NC — Redesign for Conversion & SEO Dominance

## What This Is

A complete redesign of the Breeze Roofing marketing website — transforming it from a functional contractor site into the highest-converting, best-ranking roofing website in the Wilmington NC market. The site should feel like a friendly, trusted local business (your neighbor who's great at roofing) while being engineered under the hood for #1 Google rankings across all roofing-related searches.

## Core Value

When someone in Wilmington or surrounding coastal NC towns searches for anything roofing-related, Breeze Roofing appears first — and when they land on the site, they either call or request an estimate within 60 seconds.
